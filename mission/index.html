Mission Statement

We are a group of Reading residents participating in a mentorship program to better ourselves and our community. Originally, we started this to provide a way to ease the pain of social isolation created by quarantining during the early months of the COVID-19 pandemic but we are adapting and changing to ensure that this mentorship program is available to all those who need and want it.

Our Objectives

  • Build strong and positive connections between older students and younger students.
  • Give young adults a way to take on additional responsibilities while bettering their community.
  • Create a safe place for learning, whether it's academic learning or emotional and mental growth.
